Our Services

Customized Cybersecurity Solutions for Your Business

Compliance Dashboard

Gain a centralized view of your data privacy compliance status. Track progress, identify gaps, and ensure real-time adherence to the DPDP Act with intuitive reporting tools.

Data Encryption & Security Tools

Protect sensitive data across all stages—from collection to storage and sharing. Our encryption tools provide an added layer of security, ensuring your data stays safe.

Automated Privacy Impact Assessments

Automate Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs) and ensure that your business remains compliant with the requirements of the DPDP Act.

User Consent Management

Easily manage and track user consent, ensuring that your business follows the law while respecting users' preferences for data usage

Data Breach Monitoring & Notification

Set up automated alerts and protocols in case of a data breach, ensuring timely response and reporting to avoid penalties.
